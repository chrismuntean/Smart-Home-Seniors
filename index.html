Why Choose a Smart Speaker?

Smart speakers are more than just voice-activated assistants; they can be life-saving tools in emergencies. Imagine this: if you were to fall and couldn't reach your phone, you could simply call out to your smart speaker to contact a friend, family member, or even 911.
